Common Questions

FAQ

QDo you accept brand sponsorships?
Yes! I work with outdoor brands that align with my values — hunting, fishing, gear, and the outdoor lifestyle. Reach out through the contact form.
QCan I collaborate on a video with you?
I'm open to collaborations with fellow outdoor content creators. Send a message with details about your channel and what you have in mind.
QDo you teach noodling or hunting lessons?
I don't currently offer private lessons, but all my tips, techniques, and knowledge are shared freely on my YouTube channel. Subscribe and learn along!
QWhere do you ship merch?
We ship worldwide! US orders typically arrive in 5–8 business days. International shipping takes 10–20 days. Free shipping on US orders over $75.
QHow do I get a signed photo?
Check the store for signed merchandise options, which we rotate seasonally. Follow on Instagram for giveaways and fan interaction.
QDo you respond to fan messages?
I read every message and try to respond when I can, though volume makes it tough to reply to everyone. Know that every message means the world to me.
