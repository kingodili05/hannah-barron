What I Do

From hand-grabbing catfish to tracking whitetail deer through Alabama timber — this is my world.

Noodling
Catfish noodling — reaching bare-handed into underwater holes to grab massive flathead catfish. It's dangerous, addictive, and absolutely my passion.
