My Story

Alabama Girl
Living Wild

I grew up in Crenshaw County, Alabama, surrounded by rivers, woods, and family who showed me that the outdoors isn't just a hobby — it's a way of life. My dad taught me to fish before I could read, and noodling became my obsession before I even knew it had a name.

After graduating from Troy University in 2018 with a B.S. degree, I committed fully to sharing this lifestyle with the world. What started as a few videos of me grabbing catfish out of underwater holes turned into a community of millions of people who love the outdoors as much as I do.

I'm not here to be perfect — I'm here to be real. Muddy boots, early mornings, and big catches. That's me.
